SERMON


Topic: Wait on the Lord.

Text: Isaiah 40:31

Those that wait upon the Lord shall renew their strength. If you wait for God's time then you will surely make it in life, you will not weary. You have to wait upon the Lord in:

  1. Pure Holiness
  2. Hope
  3. Strong Faith
  4. Faithfullness

Conclusion: God did not forget your case, try and put your trust in Him and He will not disappoint you. SHALOM
